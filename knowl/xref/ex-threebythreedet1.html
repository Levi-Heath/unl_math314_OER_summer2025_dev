Example 5.1.4.

Find \(\det{A}\) if
\begin{equation*} A=\begin{bmatrix}3\amp -2\amp 1\\5\amp -1\amp 2\\1\amp 4\amp 1\end{bmatrix}. \end{equation*}
Answer.
\begin{align*} \det{A}\amp =(3)\begin{vmatrix}-1\amp 2\\4\amp 1\end{vmatrix}-(-2)\begin{vmatrix}5\amp 2\\1\amp 1\end{vmatrix}+(1)\begin{vmatrix}5\amp -1\\1\amp 4\end{vmatrix} \\ \amp =(3)(-1-8)-(-2)(5-2)+(1)(20+1) \\ \amp =-27+6+21 \\ \amp =0. \end{align*}
