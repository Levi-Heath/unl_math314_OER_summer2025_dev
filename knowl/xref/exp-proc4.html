Exploration 2.2.3.

This GeoGebra interactive will allow you to go through the steps given in Algorithm 2.2.10 for a combination of vectors of your choice. To use the interactive
  1. Enter components of vectors \(\mathbf{u}\) and \(\mathbf{v}\text{.}\)
  2. Enter components of vector \(\mathbf{w}\) that you want to express as a linear combination of \(\mathbf{u}\) and \(\mathbf{v}\text{.}\)
  3. Use the navigation bar to go through the steps of Algorithm 2.2.10
