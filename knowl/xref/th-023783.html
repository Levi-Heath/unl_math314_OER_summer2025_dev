Theorem 7.4.2.

Let \(W\) be a subspace of \(\R^n\text{.}\)
  1. \(W^\perp\) is a subspace of \(\R^n\text{.}\)
  2. \(\{\mathbf{0}\}^\perp = \R^n\) and \((\R^n)^\perp = \{\mathbf{0}\}\text{.}\)
  3. If \(W = \mbox{span}\left(\mathbf{x}_{1}, \mathbf{x}_{2}, \dots, \mathbf{x}_{k}\right)\) then
    \begin{equation*} W^\perp = \{\mathbf{x} \mbox{ in } \R^n \mid \mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}_{i} = 0 \mbox{ for } i = 1, 2, \dots, k\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
