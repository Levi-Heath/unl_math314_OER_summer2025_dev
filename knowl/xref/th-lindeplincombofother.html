Theorem 2.4.12.

Let \(\left\{\mathbf{v}_1,\mathbf{v}_2,\dots ,\mathbf{v}_k\right\}\) be a set of vectors in \(\R^n\) containing two or more vectors. The following conditions are equivalent.
  1. \(\mathbf{v}_1,\mathbf{v}_2,\dots ,\mathbf{v}_k\) are linearly dependent.
  2. One of \(\mathbf{v}_1,\mathbf{v}_2,\dots ,\mathbf{v}_k\) can be expressed as a linear combination of the others.
