Theorem 3.1.23. Properties of the Transpose of a Matrix.

Let \(A\) be an \(m\times n\) matrix, \(B\) an \(n\times p\) matrix, and \(k\) a scalar. Then
  1. \(\displaystyle \left(A^{T}\right)^{T} = A\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \left( AB\right) ^{T}=B^{T}A^{T} \)
  3. \(\displaystyle \left( A+ B\right) ^{T}=A^{T}+ B^{T}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \left(kA\right)^T=kA^T\)
