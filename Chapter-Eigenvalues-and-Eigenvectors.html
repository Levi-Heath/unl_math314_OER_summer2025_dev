    Chapter 6 Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors

    Objectives

    • Define eigenvalues and eigenvectors geometrically.
    • Use characteristic polynomials to compute eigenvalues and eigenvectors.
    • Define algebraic multiplicity and geometric multiplicity.
    • Utilize properties to show that two matrices are not similar.
    • Use eigenvectors of matrices, when possible, to diagonalize a matrix.
    • Implement the Power Method and its variants.
